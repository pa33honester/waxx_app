# Privacy Policy — Waxx App

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Waxx App** ("we," "our," or "us"). Waxx App is a social commerce marketplace platform that allows users to buy products, watch live shopping streams, discover short video reels, and participate in auctions. Sellers can list products, go live, manage orders, and grow their business.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Waxx App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Full name, email address, phone number, gender, profile photo
- **Seller Information:** Business name, business tag, store details, bank account information, government ID, address proof, registration certificate (for verification)
- **Address Information:** Shipping and billing addresses, city, state, country, postal code
- **Payment Information:** We do not store card details directly. Payments are processed securely by Razorpay, Stripe, or Flutterwave
- **Communication:** Messages exchanged with sellers or buyers within the app

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device ID, device model, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, products browsed, time spent in app, search queries
- **Location Data:** Country/region (for localization and delivery purposes)
- **Camera & Media:** Only when you grant permission (for profile photos, product images, live streaming, reel creation)
- **Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics:** Crash reports and app performance data to improve stability
- **FCM Token:** Firebase Cloud Messaging token for push notifications

### 2.3 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information:
- **Firebase** (Google) — Authentication, messaging, crashlytics, analytics
- **Google Sign-In** — Account authentication
- **Apple Sign-In** — Account authentication (iOS)
- **Razorpay** — Payment processing (India)
- **Stripe** — Payment processing (international)
- **Flutterwave** — Payment processing (Africa)
- **Zego (ZEGOCLOUD)** — Live video streaming
- **Branch.io** — Deep linking and attribution
- **Socket.io** — Real-time messaging and live features

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Process orders, payments, and refunds
- Enable live selling streams and short video reels
- Send order status notifications and promotional messages
- Verify seller identities and business legitimacy
- Provide real-time chat between buyers and sellers
- Personalize product recommendations
- Detect fraud and ensure platform security
- Improve app performance via crash analytics
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We **do not sell** your personal information. We may share it with:

- **Service Providers:** Payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe, Flutterwave), cloud infrastructure, analytics providers — only to the extent necessary to provide services
- **Sellers:** Your name and shipping address are shared with sellers to fulfill your orders
- **Buyers:** Sellers' business name, profile photo, and store information are visible to buyers
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred
